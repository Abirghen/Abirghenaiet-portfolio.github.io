Hire Me

Research, interviews, and getting to the heart of the story - these motivate me as I help clear the clutter to deliver a compelling, concise message to your audience. I have 10 years of experience in public relations/communications in state government, higher education, faith-based organizations, and business - but I can research anything and become an expert in your field. As I get to know you, your business, and your audience, I will maintain your vision and unique qualities while clearing out the jargon. I'm deadline-driven and will get the job done on your timeline.In addition to writing, I have a keen eye for editing - taking a red pen to unnecessary words brings me joy! I'd be happy to help hone your letters, blogs, websites, print materials, long-form reports, and more.Finally, I have experience in project management. If you need to take a publication - such as an annual report or newsletter - from start to finish, I'm happy to collaborate with leadership and designers to make it happen.
